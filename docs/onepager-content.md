## Section 4: Examples

**Section title:**
> Here's what that looks like.

**Example 1: Menu Translator**
> **The problem:** Your menu is in one language. Your guests speak twenty.
>
> **The fix:** Take a photo of your menu. We turn it into a mobile-friendly page in any language. Put a QR code on the table. Done. Guests scan, pick their language, read your menu. No app, no download, no awkward pointing at dishes.

**Example 2: Self-Making Schedule**
> **The problem:** Every week, someone spends hours building a staff schedule by hand. Contracts, availability, preferences, labor laws — it's a puzzle that resets every Monday.
>
> **The fix:** Staff submit their availability through a simple link. The system builds the schedule automatically — respecting contracts, rest periods, peak hours, and preferences. Someone calls in sick? It finds a replacement.

**Example 3: Hotel WhatsApp Concierge**
> **The problem:** Your front desk answers the same questions 50 times a day. Checkout time, parking, room service, local restaurants. Meanwhile, the phone keeps ringing.
>
> **The fix:** A WhatsApp assistant that knows your hotel inside out. Guests ask questions, browse rooms, order room service, even pay — all in one conversation. Your staff handles the things that actually need a human.

## Design Notes

- **Tone:** Confident, direct, no fluff. Slightly conversational but professional.
- **Style:** Soft premium — warm off-whites, subtle depth, generous whitespace. See `.claude/skills/soft-premium-design/SKILL.md`
- **Animations:** Scroll-triggered fade-ins, staggered card reveals, smooth section transitions. See `.claude/skills/scroll-animations/SKILL.md`
- **Typography:** Large headlines with tight tracking, clean body text. No serif fonts.
- **No:** stock photos, robot illustrations, AI buzzwords, purple gradients, emojis
- **Yes:** clean icons (Phosphor), subtle shadows, glass effects on floating elements, whitespace
- **Mobile:** Must look great on mobile. Single column, generous padding, touch-friendly.
- **Language:** English throughout
